I am a passionate Data Analyst and Deep Learning Researcher dedicated to deriving actionable business insights and improving operational efficiency through artificial intelligence.

With a strong foundation in both data analytics and machine learning, my expertise lies in designing end-to-end AI integrations. My experience ranges from building internal automation platforms utilizing FastAPI and Airflow to developing complex deep learning models using PyTorch. I focus heavily on reducing repetitive tasks and enhancing team productivity by bridging the gap between raw data and practical, real-world applications.

From analyzing multidimensional datasets to designing sophisticated AI architectures, my goal is always to deliver scalable, high-impact solutions that drive continuous innovation.
